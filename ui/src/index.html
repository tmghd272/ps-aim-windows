Driver Mode

DS4
DualShock 4
Emulates a virtual DualShock 4 with full gyro/accel passthrough. Best for games that natively support PlayStation inputs. PS Eye tracking is not available in this mode.
🔫
Lightgun
XInput / Xbox
Emulates a virtual Xbox 360 controller. Gyro drives the right stick. PS Eye maps camera position to stick deflection from center. Best for TeknoParrot and XInput lightgun games.
🖱️
Raw Input
Mouse + KB
Emulates a mouse + keyboard. R2 = left click, left stick = WASD, face buttons = game keys. PS Eye maps the sphere directly to the screen cursor. Best for MAME and Demul lightgun games.
